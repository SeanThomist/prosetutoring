University Tutoring

I offer university tuition for Philosophy and English Literature. Many think that private tutoring ends at school, but should you want to excel in an over-subscribed university model, you must juggle institutional demands, high expectations, and often confusing writing styles. In a world where Masters degrees are becoming mandatory for professional work, you want the best results in your undergraduate degree to set yourself apart. Moreover, with excellent early results, scholarships can save you tens of thousands of dollars on post-graduate study.
